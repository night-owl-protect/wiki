# Supported Cameras

Night Owl Protect supports a wide range of Night Owl security cameras.

## Indoor Cameras

### Night Owl Indoor 1080p Wi-Fi Camera

- **Resolution**: 1080p Full HD
- **Field of View**: 110°
- **Night Vision**: 25ft infrared
- **Audio**: 2-way with noise cancellation
- **Features**: Motion detection, privacy mode

### Night Owl Indoor Pan-Tilt Camera

- **Resolution**: 1080p Full HD
- **Pan Range**: 350°
- **Tilt Range**: 115°
- **Night Vision**: 30ft infrared
- **Audio**: 2-way audio
- **Features**: Auto-tracking, patrol mode

## Outdoor Cameras

### Night Owl 4K Ultra HD Bullet Camera

- **Resolution**: 4K (3840x2160)
- **Field of View**: 100°
- **Night Vision**: 100ft with spotlight
- **Weather Rating**: IP67
- **Features**: Human/vehicle detection, color night vision

### Night Owl 4K Dome Camera

- **Resolution**: 4K (3840x2160)
- **Field of View**: 90°
- **Night Vision**: 80ft infrared
- **Weather Rating**: IP66
- **Features**: Vandal-resistant, IK10 rated

### Night Owl Spotlight Camera

- **Resolution**: 1080p Full HD
- **Spotlight**: 1000 lumens LED
- **Siren**: 100dB built-in
- **Audio**: 2-way audio
- **Features**: Active deterrence, color night vision

## Wireless Cameras

### Night Owl Wire-Free Battery Camera

- **Resolution**: 1080p Full HD
- **Battery Life**: Up to 6 months
- **Night Vision**: 33ft infrared
- **Weather Rating**: IP65
- **Features**: Solar panel compatible

### Night Owl Wire-Free Spotlight Camera

- **Resolution**: 2K (2560x1440)
- **Battery Life**: Up to 4 months
- **Spotlight**: 600 lumens
- **Weather Rating**: IP65
- **Features**: Quick-release mount

## Doorbell Cameras

### Night Owl Battery Doorbell

- **Resolution**: 1080p Full HD
- **Battery Life**: Up to 6 months
- **Audio**: 2-way audio
- **Power**: Rechargeable battery
- **Features**: Wireless installation
